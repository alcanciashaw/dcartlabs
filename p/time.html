Time

DESIGN NOTES

Time here examines the periods and intervals of work shared in the space.

Each bouncing dot represents a different student who visited the labs in a day.

Red rectangles indicate desks and tables.

The speed of the bounce represents the amount of time spent in the labs: the longer the bounce, the longer the stay.



STUDENT NOTES

Noted that the opening hours of each space are each quite different.

Noted that there was no space that was consistently open for working on projects.

Noted that students that tended to spend the most time in the lab required specialized (and expensive) equipment -- such as the paper cutters, serger sewing machines, soldering projects.

Noted that for many projects students were working on in the labs were in the early prototyping stages.
