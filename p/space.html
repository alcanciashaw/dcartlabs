DESIGN NOTES

Space here refers to the three-dimensional area that holds people and things.

This map shows the various routes students took during one afternoon in the Labs.

Here we can clearly see that space is borrowed by all students: the red paths indicate Computation Arts students and the brown paths Design students. Red rectangles indicate desks and tables.
