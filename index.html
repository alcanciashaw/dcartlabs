This website examines what is shared / borrowed in the Design and Computation Arts Labs (VC Lab, Computation Lab, Sensor Lab). These shared items can be broken down into five categories: space, knowledge, tools, resources, and time. 

These five categories are represented on a map of the 7th floor, to better understand its use and interactions.
